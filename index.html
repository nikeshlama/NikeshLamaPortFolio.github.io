StoryLine

StoryLine is an innovative Android application developed as the culmination of my MAPD course at Cambrian College. This user-centric app features an intuitive UI designed to empower users in creating and sharing their own stories. Key features of StoryLine include: Story Creation and Management: Users can draft and publish their stories directly within the app. Social Networking: The platform allows users to follow other authors, explore their stories, and build a community around storytelling. User Profiles: Each user can maintain a personalized profile showcasing their stories and interactions within the StoryLine community. StoryLine encourages creativity and community engagement, providing a platform where users can write, share, and connect through storytelling.

JobPortal

Job Portal is an Android application developed using Android Studio with the Java programming language. Leveraging Firebase for cloud storage and user authentication, the app facilitates a seamless user experience. Users can register as either Job Providers or Job Seekers, enabling Job Providers to post job listings while assisting Job Seekers in finding relevant opportunities through categorized job filters. Additionally, integrated chat functionality fosters direct communication between Job Seekers and Job Providers, enhancing networking and engagement within the platform.

PetCare

PetCare is an Android application designed for pet owners to effectively manage their pets' health needs. Developed to operate seamlessly on Android platforms, PetCare serves as a comprehensive tool for scheduling and tracking vaccinations and checkups. The app facilitates timely appointments with veterinary professionals and ensures pet owners receive notifications with detailed appointment information. Moreover, PetCare integrates chat functionality, enabling convenient communication between pet owners and veterinary providers for personalized pet care management.
